**Quantum AI & Federated Learning Researcher | Assistant Professor**

## 🎓 About Me
I am a researcher and academic specializing in the intersection of **Federated Learning, Quantum Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity**. I recently completed my PhD at the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at the **National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (Taiwan Tech)**. 

My work is driven by the goal of empowering Industrial IoT and Edge Computing through secure, distributed, and quantum-enhanced AI systems.

* **PhD Thesis:** *Federated Learning for Explainable Intrusion Detection in Edge IoT: Leveraging Neural Boosting Ensembles, Hybrid Quantum-Classical Models, and Sparse Mixtures of Quantum Experts.*
* **Core Focus:** Federated Learning, Quantum AI, Explainable AI (XAI), Edge Computing, and Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS).
* **Looking Ahead:** I am actively exploring new research avenues in Quantum Machine Learning and Cybersecurity, and I am highly open to collaborations and new opportunities.
